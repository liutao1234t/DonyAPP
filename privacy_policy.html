Privacy At A Glance

Dony is designed for dog styling, AI photo transformations, grooming chat, and saved inspiration. We collect only what is reasonably needed to provide those functions, keep the service safe, and support you when you contact us.

Non-negotiable privacy statementWe do not sell, trade, rent, or share user information. We do not collect information unrelated to Dony’s functions. We strictly protect user privacy.

What We May Process

Photos and prompts

Dog photos, selected style presets, and prompt text may be processed when you ask Dony to create a transformation or style suggestion.

Messages and care questions

AI Groomer chats and direct-style messages may be processed to provide relevant grooming tips, care ideas, or conversation continuity.

App operation data

We may process limited device, crash, credit, feature usage, and diagnostic data needed to run, secure, debug, and improve Dony.

Support contact

If you email us, we process your email address and message so we can respond.

What We Do Not Do

No unrelated collection

We do not ask for or collect information that is not connected to Dony’s pet styling, chat, account, safety, payment, or support functions.

No data sales

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share user information for advertising brokers or unrelated commercial databases.

No third-party AI models

Dony’s AI models are self-developed, self-trained, and self-hosted by us. We do not use third-party AI model services.

No hidden profile building

We do not build unrelated profiles from your pet photos, grooming chats, or style choices.

How We Use Information

We use information to generate AI pet looks, answer grooming questions, display style inspiration, manage credits, maintain account features, prevent abuse, fix bugs, provide support, and comply with legal obligations.

When we improve Dony, we focus on feature quality, safety, reliability, and user experience. We do not use personal information for unrelated tracking or sale.

Storage And Protection

Limited retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purpose described here, unless a longer period is required for security, legal, billing, or dispute reasons.

Security controls

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, and disclosure.

User control

You may contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy help, subject to verification and applicable law.

Children And Sensitive Data

Dony is not intended to collect sensitive personal information. Please do not submit health records, government IDs, financial details, or private information about other people. If you believe a child provided personal information without proper permission, contact us.
